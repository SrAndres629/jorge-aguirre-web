# Skill: CRM Maestro

Masterful management of customer relationships, loyalty, and LTV optimization.

## Instructions
1.  **Context**: Always read the user's transaction and interaction history before making suggestions.
2.  **Logic**: Use the `segmentation_engine.py` to classify users into RFM tiers.
3.  **Automation**: Generate tailored communication templates (Email/WhatsApp) based on the specific behavior trigger (e.g., "Abandoned Cart", "Power User", "At-Risk").
4.  **Audit**: Ensure all data handling complies with the privacy standards defined in `CRM_MAESTRO.md`.

## Sub-Components
-   **Segmentation Engine**: `segmentation_engine.py`
-   **Loyalty Dashboard**: `loyalty_calculator.js`

## Metrics of Impact
-   **LTV Growth**: Goal +15% per quarter.
-   **Churn Reduction**: Goal -5% monthly for the "At-Risk" segment.
-   **Engagement Score**: Target > 8.0/10 across active users.
